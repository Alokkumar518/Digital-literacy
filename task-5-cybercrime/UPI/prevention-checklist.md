# Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students 

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Always verify UPI payment requests; do not approve “collect requests” from unknown sources.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
- Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.
- Review privacy settings on social media and limit sharing of personal information.
- Log out from shared or public computers after use.
- Regularly monitor your bank statements and transaction history for any unauthorized activity.
